About The Company

Capture is a photography company founded by Manfred "Marley" Edun. It was created to provide people with everlasting memories through photographs. The idea is to capture people at their happiest moments, usually unscripted and in the moment.

About the Founder

Manfred "Marley" Edun

Hi, its Marley here. I am the founder and CEO of Capture Photography. I am a software engineer and a photographer. I created this company to be able to express myself through my photographs and to help people tell their stories through photographs.

Types of Camera

  • DSLR Camera: DSLR camera is the short form of Digital Single Lens Reflex. It comes with a detachable lens so it can be switched based on the type of photography.
  • Mirrorless Camera: You can get both Crop frame camera bodies as well as full frame camera bodies in mirrorless cameras.
  • Point and Shoot Camera: Point and Shoot cameras are compact cameras, useful for people who want to capture vacation pictures or family pictures.
